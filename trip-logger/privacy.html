Privacy Policy

Enocean Tours Trip Logger · Last updated June 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Enocean Tours LLC ("Enocean Tours", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with the Enocean Tours Trip Logger application and related website (together, the "Service"). The Service is a tool used by tour operators and their crew to log trips, record wildlife sightings, and send trip reports to guests. By using the Service you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect

  • Account information. When a captain, crew member, or guest signs in, we collect an email address and an encrypted password used to authenticate the account.
  • Location data. While a trip is active, we collect precise GPS location from the device to record the vessel's route and calculate distance traveled. See the section below for details.
  • Trip and sighting data. Wildlife sightings (species, counts, times, and notes), photos, audio recordings, environmental conditions (such as water temperature and sea state), and passenger counts that crew enter during a trip.
  • Booking information. When a booking is made through a connected booking provider (FareHarbor), we receive booking details such as the customer's name, email address, phone number, party size, amounts paid, and survey responses, so crew can match guests to the trip they are running.
  • Technical data. Standard server and device information such as IP address and request logs, collected automatically to operate and secure the Service.

Location data

Trip Logger collects precise location, including in the background (while the app is closed or the screen is locked), only while a trip is active. We use it solely to record the vessel's route and calculate distance traveled for that trip, so the breadcrumb path stays accurate for the full duration even when the phone is locked in a pocket. Location collection stops when the trip ends.

We do not use location data for advertising, and we do not sell it. You can turn off location access at any time in your device settings, though doing so will prevent the app from recording an accurate route.

How we use information

  • To operate the Service and let crew log trips and record sightings.
  • To calculate trip distance and draw the vessel's route from the recorded location track.
  • To generate and send trip reports to guests at the email addresses provided.
  • To pre-fill trip details (such as passenger count) from connected booking information.
  • To display an operator's public sightings widget, which an operator may configure to hide precise map coordinates.
  • To secure, maintain, and improve the Service.

How information is shared

We do not sell personal information. We share information only with service providers that process data on our behalf to run the Service, and only as needed for that purpose:

  • Supabase (database, authentication, and file storage hosting).
  • Vercel (application and website hosting).
  • FareHarbor (the booking provider that sends us booking information).
  • Email delivery providers (such as Google) used to send trip reports and account emails.

We may also disclose information if required by law, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users and the Service.

Data storage and security

Information is stored on managed cloud infrastructure (Supabase) protected by access controls and row level security so that each operator can access only their own data. Passwords are stored in encrypted form. While no system is perfectly secure, we take reasonable measures to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Data retention

We keep trip, sighting, and booking records for as long as the operator's account is active and as needed to provide the Service, unless a longer period is required by law. You may request deletion of your data as described below.

Your choices and rights

  • You can turn off location access in your device settings at any time.
  • You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we hold about you by contacting us.
  • Guests can ask us to remove their email address and associated trip report data.

Children

The Service is intended for tour operators and their crew and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the Service after a change means you accept the updated policy.

Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to make a privacy request, contact us at enoceantours@gmail.com.
