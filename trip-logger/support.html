Common questions

Location tracking. The app records your route by GPS only while a trip is active, including in the background so the route stays accurate while the screen is locked. Tracking stops when you end the trip. You can manage location access in your device Settings.

Account access. An operator account is required to use the app. If you cannot sign in, contact us at the email above and we will help restore access.
